The Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore will be celebrating National Science Day with a showcase of ongoing research and development. Like last year, OPEN DAY 2011 will be a campus-wide event where one may visit exhibits put up at each department. Unlike last year however, Open Day 2011 will be a two day event, which should be sufficient time to view the exhibits at all the departments.
