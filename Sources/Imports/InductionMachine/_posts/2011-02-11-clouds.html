Or rather the lack thereog; which is noteworthy... The Bangalore night sky is perfectly clear - ideal for stargazing.

Beginners may want to check out a nice piece of software called Stellarium - a virtual planetarium on your PC. Both linux and windows versions are available. Debian/Ubuntu users can use "apt-get install". Be warned, though, that a graphics card is recommended!
