Yesterday there was an exhibition of electric  cars participating in a zero emissions race - Wave 2011. The expo was organised by CEDT (Indian Institute of Science) and Swissnex.



There were four teams, driving four different electric cars - The Renault Twingo, the REVA, the Twix, and the Interlaken. An interesting post by one of the drivers details how to get the maximum juice out of an electric vehicle when travelling on the highways.
