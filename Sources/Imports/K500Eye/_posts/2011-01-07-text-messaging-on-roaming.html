--- layout: post title: Text Messaging on Roaming date: '2011-01-07T11:36:00.000+05:30' author: Abhijit Kshirsagar tags: - sms - roaming - circuit switched modified_time: '2011-01-07T11:36:28.341+05:30' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2880836941511398370.post-2921392168148752216 blogger_orig_url: https://thek550eye.blogspot.com/2011/01/text-messaging-on-roaming.html --- If you find that your text messaging service (SMS) starts acting up when you are roaming, try the following:

From the Main Menu, go to
Settings > Connectivity > Data Comm. > SMS Access
and set it to Circuit Switched.


This should work for most Sony Phones. For other phones use your discretion!
